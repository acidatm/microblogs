nomadic minimalism

owning only as much as needed

owning replaceble concepts instead of things

detaching oneself from objects

enabling oneself to move freely

  • bed in a box and rollable futon

    the german company bed in a box offers cheap, packable and lightweight bed frames made from re-enforced cardboard. combining this bedframe with a rollable futon should provide a very movable alternative to a classic bedframe with mattress.

  • ikea classics

    ikea carries some items which they refer to as their “killer”, in every category there is one item which is priced so low that it is impossible to compete. with ikea being such a global enterprise its products are widely available on the second-hand market. products such as the poäng arm chair, lack side table, frösvi and stefan chairs and the kallax and hyllis shelving units can be had for very cheap around the world.
